Bio

I obtained my PhD degree from UIUC, advised by Professor Carl A. Gunter. I received my B.S. degree and M.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong Univerisity.
My main research interests span the broad area of cybersecurity, with a focus on Internet of Things (IoT) security, machine learning (ML) security, and system security. I also have broad research interests in distributed systems, software engineering and formal methods.
